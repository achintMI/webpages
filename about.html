About ITS

The Department of Information and Technology Services (ITS) works to build the capacity of schools to use information and technology, maintain network infrastructure, security standards, and support other departments to meet district goals in order to prepare our students for success in college and careers. We provide innovative technology solutions that improve the quality of education for students, reduce the administrative burden on educators, facilitate parent interaction, increase community engagement, and support CPS’ mission of transparency by focusing on the ease and equity of access to information.

Our Impact
We make a difference across the District by connecting families, schools, staff, and community members to the information they need.

Our Commitments
We are committed to serving educators, families and community members by supporting CPS' mission of providing a high-quality public education for every child, in every neighborhood.

Support

Provide our educators and leaders with the technology and information required to support quality instruction for our students.

Equip

Ensure all students across the District have equitable access to the technology resources to be college and career ready.

Simplify

Reduce both the complexity of and time spent by our educators and leaders navigating our systems, processes and support services.

Cultivate

Cultivate a sustainable culture of service, leadership and support by investing in our amazing people and providing the resources they need to succeed.

Enable

Improve the tools we use to communicate to and between our educators, leaders, parents and community members.

Our Goals

Goal 1: Deliver ITS Portfolio

Goal 2: Deliver Innovative Digital K-12 Capabilities

Goal 3: Champion K-12 Educational and IT Industry Standards

Goal 4: Drive Flawless Project Execution

Goal 5: Continuous CPS Blueprint and IT Strategy Alignment

Goal 6: Build a High-Performance Organization (HPO)

CPS Business Transformation

CPS’ business transformation program will transform our existing Financial and HR Systems into a cloud-based framework.
